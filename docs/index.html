About ReMind

ReMind is a mobile app that helps you stay on top of your goals by sending text messages that you write for yourself. Messages are only sent to users who voluntarily provide their phone number and consent during onboarding in the app.

Use case category: Customer Care / Productivity (self-authored reminders).

How Users Opt In (In‑App)

Users opt in during onboarding inside the ReMind app. They enter their phone number and agree to receive reminder texts. Below is the exact consent language used in the app:

By tapping Agree, you consent to receive reminder text messages from ReMind to the phone number you provide. Message & data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your settings and activity. Reply STOP to opt out, or HELP for support.
