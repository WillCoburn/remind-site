Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-24

ReMind (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides a mobile application that allows users to create and receive personal reminder text messages. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact Data: Phone number you provide, and optional email if you contact support.
  • Reminder Content: Reminder titles and scheduling metadata you create. You control this content; we process it to deliver reminders.
  • Technical Data: Log and diagnostic data such as device type, app version, and approximate usage analytics.

How We Use Information

  • To deliver reminder text messages and app functionality
  • To provide support and respond to inquiries
  • To maintain safety, prevent abuse, and comply with legal requirements
  • To improve our services and user experience

SMS Messaging

By consenting in the app, you agree to receive SMS messages from ReMind. Message frequency varies based on your settings and activity. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your phone number or reminder content to third parties. We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., SMS delivery providers) to operate the service, subject to appropriate confidentiality and data processing terms.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP
  • Request access or deletion by emailing support@yourdomain.com
  • Control app settings to manage reminder frequency

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no system can be 100% secure.

Children

ReMind is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, contact us to remove it.

International Users

Our service is intended for use in the United States.
