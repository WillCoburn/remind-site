Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-25

ReMind (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides a mobile application that allows users to create and receive personal reminder text messages. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Phone number you provide to create an account, and optional email if you contact support.
  • User Content: Affirmations and reminders you submit. You control this content; we store and process it to deliver reminders back to you.
  • Technical Data: Device type, app version, usage logs, and diagnostic information to maintain the service.
  • Payment Data: If you subscribe, billing and payment information is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your full payment details.

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver reminder text messages and app functionality
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To maintain safety, prevent abuse, and comply with legal requirements
  • To improve our services and user experience

3. SMS Messaging

By consenting in the app, you agree to receive SMS messages from ReMind. Message frequency varies based on your activity and settings. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your information. We may share limited data with service providers solely to operate the Service:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, cloud storage, and analytics
  • Twilio: SMS message delivery
  • App stores (Apple, Google): subscriptions and billing

These providers process data on our behalf under confidentiality and data-processing agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us.
